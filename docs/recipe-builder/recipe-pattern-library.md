# Recipe Pattern Library

Use these patterns as reference defaults when generating new Weeknight Table recipes.

## Fried Rice Pattern

Default:

- Chicken breast.
- Microwave jasmine rice pouches or frozen cooked rice.
- Bottled stir-fry sauce.
- Frozen peas and carrots.
- Eggs.
- Scallions and garlic.
- Optional ginger.

Key notes:

- Use 3 standard rice pouches for the household.
- Break up rice before stir-frying.
- Let moist rice dry briefly in the pan.
- Keep sauce light at first.
- Pull kid portions before extra sauce or adult seasoning.

## Steak Frites Pattern

Default:

- Steak such as strip, sirloin, ribeye, hanger, or skirt.
- Frozen fries in the air fryer.
- Pan sauce from steak drippings.
- Optional steak rub.
- No fresh herbs by default.

Key notes:

- Do not overcrowd air-fryer fries.
- Rest steak before slicing.
- Slice skirt or hanger steak against the grain.
- Sauce stays on the side for kids.
- Frozen fries are a useful shortcut, not a compromise.

## Sausage And Greens Pasta Pattern

Default:

- Orecchiette or another short pasta.
- Mild Italian pork sausage.
- Broccoli rabe.
- Shallots caramelized in pork fond.
- Butter, garlic, pasta water, and grated cheese.

Key notes:

- Blanch broccoli rabe to reduce bitterness.
- Reserve pasta water before blanching if pasta is cooked first.
- Add garlic after shallots so it does not burn.
- Spicy sausage is optional.
- Air-fried broccoli is a backup green, not the first choice.
- Set aside pasta and sausage before adding broccoli rabe if needed for kids.

## Chicken Piccata Pattern

Default:

- Thin chicken breast cutlets.
- Pasta as the base.
- Lemon-caper-butter pan sauce.
- Chicken broth default.
- Wine optional.
- Parsley optional.

Key notes:

- Capers stay; they define the dish.
- Shake excess flour off chicken cutlets.
- Use cold butter to gloss the sauce.
- Make enough sauce for pasta.
- Keep some plain chicken aside before saucing for kids.

## Taco Kit Night Pattern

Default:

- Hard-and-soft taco kit.
- 1 lb ground beef.
- Small yellow rice packet.
- Shredded Mexican cheese.
- Shredded lettuce.
- Sour cream.
- Optional salsa, tomatoes, avocado, or guacamole.

Key notes:

- Yellow rice is important, not optional.
- Build-your-own serving is the kid-flex.
- Follow kit seasoning and water directions, but simmer until glossy, not watery.
- Keep toppings separate.
